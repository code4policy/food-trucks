Submit Your Food Truck Permit Application:

Once you have all the necessary permits and licenses ready, you can submit your permit application:

You can apply Online, by E-mail, or in Person!

📄 What You Need:



✅

Copy of Hawkers and Peddlers Licences

✅

Copy of Business Certificate

✅

Copy of Fire Permit (Open Burning/Cooking Permit)

✅

Copy of Health Permit

✅

Proof Of Service By Commissary Kitchen

✅

Copy of Premise Permits

- including lease and/or letter from landlord(s).

💲 What You Pay

There’s a $500 annual health permit fee.
