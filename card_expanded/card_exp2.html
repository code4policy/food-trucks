Hawker and Peddler License

Each worker who handles money on a food truck needs to get a hawker and peddler license through the state. They need to renew the license every year.

✅

Hawker and Peddler license form

Fill in the Hawker and Peddler license form for each worker.

✅

Contact local police

Have the local police chief to sign Certificate of Character section of the form. The police check each applicant's criminal history through their fingerprints

✅

Send your form

Either bring in or mail your applications and payments to
Director of Standards

Boston, MA 02108
