Get a Fire Permit

To operate your food truck in Boston, you must schedule a fire inspection

✅

Schedule your inspection!

Call the Fire Department to schedule your inspection for a fire permit at 617-343-3447. They do inspections Monday through Friday, from 8 a.m. - 11 a.m.

✅

On the day of your inpsection, bring:

  • Your food truck permit
  • Your finalized food truck

    • to Fire Prevention Office 1010 Massachusetts Avenue, Boston, MA 02218

✅

If you anticipate using anything but an inline generator for cooking or to power your truck, you will need to bring:

  • A completed Open Burning and Cooking application
  • To get this permit, you will need a fire suppression system and a fire extinguisher.
    • Upon passing the fire inspection, you will receive your permit, and the fire inspector will sign your food truck permit application!

    💲 What you pay

    The fee for a fire inspection is $150
