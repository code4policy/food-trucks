Step 3: 3333:

Before you start building your food truck, you should obtain necessary documents.

📄 What You Need

⏱️ Estimated Time

1-2 weeks

✅

Schedule a review of building plan

You should go over the plans for your truck with Inspectional Services before you start building. You can schedule an appointment to review your construction and operation plans by calling 617-961-3293.

✅

Bring the necessary documents to the review

  • Equipment plans and specifications for your truck
  • Food truck plan review form to find out what other permits you’ll need
  • A completed health permit application
  • A sample menu to help you figure out the layout of the kitchen
✅

Go to the review

💲 What You Pay

There’s a $100 annual health permit fee. If you pay the fee during your review, you will not have to pay later when your truck is inspected.
