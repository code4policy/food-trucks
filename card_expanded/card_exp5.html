Proof of Commissary with a licensed kitchen

We need a letter from the owner that states you use the facility as your base of operations. If the kitchen is outside of Boston, you need to include that City or town's health permit for the commissary kitchen with your letter.

✅

Proof that at least one person on your truck is a Certified Food Manager

This includes your:

  • Allergen Certification
  • ServSafe Certificate

💲 What You Pay

There’s a $100 annual health permit fee. If you pay the fee during your review, you will not have to pay later when your truck is inspected.
