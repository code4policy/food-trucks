Procure Insurance

The City of Boston requires your food truck to be insured before the wheel can start rolling.

✅

Contact insurance provider

Contact and purchase insurance according to your needs from a provider that offers coverage for food trucks.

✅

Bring the necessary documents to the review

Once the policy is active, request a certificate of liability insurance from your insurance agent or company.

✅

Request certificate

Obtain the Certificate of Liability Insurance from your provider (example of the Certificate)


Be sure to use Boston City Hall Address (1 City Hall Square #500 Boston, MA 02201) as Certificate Holder.

💲 What You Pay

Depend on the insurance you buy
